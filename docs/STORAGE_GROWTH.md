# Storage Growth

## Baseline
- **Documents**: 100GB (1M documents)
- **Vectors**: ~50GB
- **Graph**: ~20GB
- **Metadata**: ~10GB

## Growth Rate
- **Daily Ingestion**: 500 documents (~50MB)
- **Monthly Growth**: ~1.5GB Raw + ~1GB Derived Data

## Retention Policy
- Logs: 30 days
- Traces: 7 days
- Processed Jobs: 90 days
